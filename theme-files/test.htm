Seller's Manual

Your step-by-step guide to mastering the AYAAM Dashboard

1. Getting Started

Login to Dashboard

Use your email and password to sign in and access your seller dashboard.

Update Profile

Set up your shop name, logo, and contact details to personalize your store.

Store Settings

Adjust shipping zones, payment methods, and policies from settings.

2. Managing Products

Add, edit, and organize your product catalog with ease.

  • Add Product: Go to Products → Add New.
  • Edit Product: Click any product from your list and update details.
  • Delete/Archive: Remove products no longer available.

3. Orders & Sales

Track and manage all customer orders from one place.

  • View Orders: Orders tab shows all new and past orders.
  • Update Status: Mark orders as Processing, Shipped, Delivered.
  • Returns/Refunds: Handle requests quickly to build trust.

4. Payments & Earnings

Stay on top of your earnings.

  • Check Balance: Dashboard → Earnings overview.
  • Withdraw: Request payouts to your linked account.
  • Schedule: Payouts are processed every week.

5. Support & Help

Need help? We’ve got you covered.

  • FAQs: Visit the Help Center in your dashboard.
  • Contact Us: Reach our support team anytime.
